Front-End Development:
I craft intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces using modern frameworks like React and Vue.js. Leveraging technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, I focus on building responsive and accessible designs that perform flawlessly across devices.

DevOps & Deployment:
I utilize tools like Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines to streamline deployment and ensure reliable application performance. Hosting services like AWS, Google Cloud, and Netlify allow me to deploy scalable solutions tailored to specific business needs.

Back-End Development:
My back-end expertise includes developing secure, high-performance servers and APIs using Node.js, Express, and Django. I ensure data integrity and efficiency by designing and managing databases with MySQL, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL.

Web Designer

As a web designer, I combine functionality and aesthetics to create user-friendly websites that leave a lasting impression. My approach emphasizes clarity, simplicity, and alignment with brand identity.

UI/UX Design:

I design wireframes and prototypes using tools like Figma and Adobe XD, ensuring a user-centric approach to interface design. By focusing on usability, I help businesses increase engagement and achieve their goals.

Responsive Web Design:

All my websites are optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. I prioritize clean code and lightweight designs to enhance performance and search engine optimization (SEO).

Content Management Systems (CMS):

I customize websites using WordPress, enabling clients to manage content effortlessly. I also integrate plugins and develop custom themes for unique functionality.

Highlight Projects:

  • Informational websites for NGOs with donation and volunteer management systems.
  • Interactive blogs with seamless content management.
